What are the world's fastest cars?

Ever since the days of Henry Ford cars have been produced, but that does not mean all cars are the same or have the same function. Some cars are built for pure speed, this page is going to take a look at what these cars are, include additional information and text and meet requirements for Epicodus Intro to Programming project of week 1. So lets take a look at criteria to be on this list of 5 of the fastest cars in the year 2017.

Criteria:

  • Car must be a production car(not a racecar)
  • Car must be availible for purchase in 2017
  • Top speed must have been tested on similar road conditions
  • The car must be fully powered by itself and not using additional speed boosting circumstances such as driving down a hill.

Here is a list of the 5 fastest cars in 2017 along with top speed:

  1. 2017 Bugatti Chiron: 261 MPH
  2. 2018 Aston Martin Valkyrie: 250 MPH
  3. 2018 Lamborghini Aventador S: 217 MPH
  4. 2017 Ford GT: 216 MPH
  5. 2018 McLaren 720S: 212 MPH
